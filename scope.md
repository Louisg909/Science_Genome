
# Scope of project

The intent of this file is to help ensure that the project work and write up stays scoped and bounded. Directional to what it is actually trying to solve. Not going down tangents, and not ticking along without making progress on the goals.

## Personal intent
To get the paper published and learn the pipeline of research that merges abstract ideas and computation.

## Project intent
To formalise the idea of having this transformer and citation tree initiated genetic flows and showing its usefulness in Scientometrics and studying how ideas and papers evolve over time. Not a complete exploration that comes with insightful results, but opening the framework and proving it could have insightful results.
