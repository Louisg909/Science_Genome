\begin{abstract}
This document is the final research artifact for the Science Genome project. It defines a complete, standalone framework for analyzing how scientific ideas are inherited, transformed, and recombined across publication lineages. The framework combines semantic representations of papers with time-respecting citation structure to separate inherited content from residual contribution. It specifies model assumptions, estimation constraints, interpretation rules, and verification-and-validation requirements so outputs can be scrutinized as research evidence rather than treated as opaque scores. The artifact also formalizes scope boundaries, quality criteria, and reproducibility expectations needed to use the framework as a publishable and inspectable basis for idea-flow analysis.
\end{abstract}
